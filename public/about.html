Meet Dr. Cindy Sonnier

Dr. Cindy Sonnier is a licensed clinical social worker and founder of RayAnn Care Psychological Services. With over a decade of experience, she specializes in psychological evaluations, brief therapy, and professional coaching, all through the lens of cultural competence and compassionate care.

"I created RayAnn Care to offer accessible, ethical, and empowering mental health services to diverse communities across the U.S."

Dr. Sonnier’s approach combines clinical excellence with clarity and warmth. Whether you are facing a life transition, pursuing immigration support, or building a private practice, she ensures every interaction is affirming, informative, and focused on your well-being./p>
